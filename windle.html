FAQ

  • info_outlineFinding your station number

    Watch the video above or follow the following instructions:

    Go to Windfinder.com and click on the "More" button on the top right of the page.

    Then select "Weather widget" under "Services" and create a widget for your preffered weather station (! Make sure that the station has wind reports ! )

    On step 3 during the configuration you will see the html code. In that code look for STATIONSNR: ****

    The **** is your StationNR to enter in the watchapps configuration page.

  • info_outlineWhere is the configuration page?

    In your Pebble App (where you manage watchfaces and watchapps ) go to the Apps tab.

    Look for Windle and click on the gear icon or click on the app and then "Settings"

  • info_outlineThe settings wont update!

    Due to the Clay (configuration page) inplementation in PebbleJS you need to restart the app on the watch after you saved the settings.

  • info_outlineApp hangs at "Loading..."

    Windle connects to the internet to get the current windspeeds.

    Depending on your connection to the internet (Wifi, LTE etc) it may take a few seconds to load the data.

    If the app hangs longer than 30 seconds, exit the app and check your phones internet connection. Then try again.

    If it still doesn't work, check on the wind reports page of your configured station on Windfinder.com, if there are no wind reports the app will not work.
